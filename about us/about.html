ABOUT US

✅ 1. Who We Are
> ZAMRENT is a modern online housing platform built for Zambians by Zambians. We connect renters, landlords, and property agents across the country through a simple and reliable website.


✅ 2. Our Mission > Our mission is to make house-hunting easier, faster, and more transparent in Zambia. Whether you're looking for a single room or a furnished mansion, we’ve got you covered.

✅ 3. What We Offer
🏡 Browse verified rental properties by city, price, or size.
🔍 Advanced search filters to narrow your options.
📷 High-quality images and accurate details for every listing.
📱 Mobile-friendly experience so you can search on the go.
👨‍💼 Landlord and agent portal to upload and manage listings.


✅ 4. Why Choose ZAMRENT?
🇿🇲 Local Focus: We understand the Zambian housing market.
✅ Trustworthy Listings: We verify every property to avoid scams.
⚡ Fast & Easy: Find and contact landlords in just a few clicks.
🤝 Community Support: We're always improving based on user feedback.


✅ 5. Our Story (Optional, but builds connection)
> ZAMRENT was started by a group of young developers and housing professionals who saw how difficult it was to find reliable housing information in Zambia.
We believe everyone deserves a safe and affordable place to live — and finding one shouldn’t be complicated.
